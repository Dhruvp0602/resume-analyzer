FAQs

What is SkillScan and how does it work?

SkillScan is an AI-powered resume analyzer that evaluates your resume, extracts key skills, checks ATS compatibility, and provides personalized career improvement suggestions. Simply upload your resume, and the system generates a detailed analysis report within seconds.

Is SkillScan free to use?

Yes. SkillScan offers a free version that allows users to upload their resume and receive basic skill analysis and improvement suggestions. Advanced features may be included in future premium plans.

What file formats are supported?

SkillScan currently supports: PDF (.pdf) Word Documents (.docx) More formats may be added in future updates.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

SkillScan uses intelligent text extraction and natural language processing techniques to identify skills, keywords, and experience. While it provides strong insights, users should review suggestions carefully before making final resume changes.

Can SkillScan guarantee I will get a job?

No tool can guarantee a job. However, SkillScan helps optimize your resume, identify skill gaps, and improve ATS compatibility — significantly increasing your chances of landing interviews.

Is my resume data secure?

Yes. Your resume is processed securely and is not shared with third parties. User privacy and data protection are a top priority for SkillScan.
